### Project description: 
The Placement Management System is a comprehensive web-based platform designed to streamline the recruitment process between universities, companies, and students. The system facilitates efficient placement coordination through four key modules: Admin (University), Companies (Job Providers), Tutors, and Students. Universities can manage tutors and verify registered entities, companies can post job opportunities with specific skill requirements, tutors guide students by matching them with suitable job postings, and students can apply for positions aligned with their skills and certifications. The system ensures a structured workflow from registration through job application, interview scheduling, and offer management, ultimately tracking student placement status while preventing multiple job acceptances.

### List of requirements (objectives): 

Essential:
- University admin login with default credentials and ability to add tutors with secure credentials
- Student and company registration system with email verification (university email required for students)
- Admin approval workflow for company and student registration requests
- Company job posting functionality including job title, description, candidate requirements, application dates, required skills, and job category
- Tutor interface to view job requirements and assign suitable students based on skills matching
- Student profile management with skills and certifications
- Student job application system for tutor-assigned positions
- Company application review and interview scheduling system
- Real-time interview round updates and scheduled date notifications
- Automated notification system for selected and rejected candidates
- Offer letter generation and distribution by companies
- Multi-role offer letter viewing (university, tutors, students)
- Student offer acceptance/rejection functionality
- Placement status tracking to prevent students from applying to other drives once placed
- Feedback system for companies, tutors, and students with public visibility

Desirable:
- Offer letter tracking dashboard for universities and tutors showing acceptance/rejection status
- Interview rescheduling functionality requiring dual approval from university and company
- Working status management by university and tutors for placed students
- Enhanced reporting and analytics on placement outcomes

Optional:
- Real-time chat functionality between stakeholders (student-company, student-tutor, company-admin)
- Comprehensive push notification system for all platform updates
- Automatic placement drive restriction for students with accepted offers
- Calendar integration for interview scheduling
- Automated skill-based job matching algorithm
- Document management system for resumes and offer letters
